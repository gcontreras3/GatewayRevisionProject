About Mykonos

The city is a fun luxurious Mediterranean location and that of a cycladic island with sugar-cube houses, traditional windmills, visually attractive churches, and winding pathways.
Mykonos's nickname is "The Island of the Winds", due to the very strong winds that usually blow on the island.

The Currency

The currency used in Mykonos is the Euro. 1 US Dollar = 0.9 Euros.


Language spoken

The official language in Mykonos is Greek. However, because it is such a touristy island, you can get away with using English.
